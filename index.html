Fitness Center

State of the art fitness facility providing CCL students and faculty a space to exercise, socialize, and unwind. The fitness center is a breathtaking space, filled with natural light streaming from floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking Columbus. The atmosphere is energizing with upbeat music filling the air and trainers offering personalized attention. The space is meticulously maintained, creating an inviting and comfortable environment for anyone looking to achieve their fitness goals.

CCL offers both instructor-led group fitness classes and personal training sessions to help members achieve their fitness goals.
